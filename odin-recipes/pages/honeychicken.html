Honey Sesame Chicken

Description:

Honey sesame chicken typically has a sweet, savory flavor with a prominent honey taste balanced by the nutty richness of toasted sesame oil, often with a slightly crunchy texture from the sesame seeds, making it a sticky, flavorful dish that's considered both sweet and savory; it's commonly found in Chinese takeout menus.

Ingredients:

Chicken:

  • 1 lb skinless boneless chicken thighs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/3 cup vegetable oil
  • 1/2 green onion

Sauce:

  • 1/2 cup honey
  • 2.5 tablespoon regular soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon white granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 2 teaspoon Shaoxing Cooking Wine
  • 2 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 3/4 cup water

Steps:

  1. In a small bowl, combine the sauce ingredients as listed and set aside.

  2. Dice chicken thighs into 1.5-inch pieces and transfer to a large bowl. Season diced chicken with salt.

  3. Add cornstarch to the diced chicken and evenly coat each piece. Feel free to add more cornstarch if needed. Set aside.

  4. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan on medium-high heat. Fry coated chicken in small batches until golden brown and crispy, about 6-8 minutes in total, flipping halfway. Remove and transfer fried chicken to a wire rack or a paper towel lined plate.

  5. Remove any excess oil in the pan and pour the sauce to simmer until thickened.

  6. Toss in fried chicken into sauce until evenly coated. Remove off heat. Garnish with green onions. Enjoy!
